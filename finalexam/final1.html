What are birds?

Birds are winged animals. They are warmblooded and lay eggs instead of giving birth to live young. They are the closest living relative of dinosaurs. Ornithology is the study of birds!

Classification

All birds follow the same classification hierarchy, with some variations in order, family, genus, and species.

  • Domain: Eukarya
  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Class: Aves

Basic Anatomy

All birds have the same basic structures, including eyes, a beak, wings, and feet.
